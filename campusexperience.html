Our Values

Kids will uncover the best version of themselves. We advocate for them to become self-aware, thoughtful, and compassionate people. We create strong social and emotional bonds with them while inspiring them to take risks. We upskill them to master self-balance, open-mindedness, and methods to indulge in effective communication.

Our team works on academic skills through personalised coaching and educational tools like Let’s Flip and . We bring together the skills, techniques, and practices that can aid them to thrive academically. We also focus on independence, curiosity, organisation, timeliness, personalization, effectiveness, and efficiency.

We provide enrichment programs for kid's to gain mastery and skills which can be applied throughout their lives. Some of the lifelong skills we teach are Design Thinking, Entrepreneurship, Creative Writing, Situational Thinking/Computational Thinking and Learning Experience Design.

Ensure physical health by training with coaches who have hands-on involvement with skills training. The list consists of running techniques, tactical plays, ball handling and sports such as netball, football and frisbee. Self organised nation-wide competitions and friendly games are included!

Extending into one of the most vital components of a child’s life, an environment suited towards improving mental health. Our coaches are present in mental health and relationship checks to ensure a much better understanding of yourself and of those around you.

A safe space for the youth. Kid's are able to avoid judgement and ridicule by engaging in vulnerable, respectful, challenging and honest conversations about various topics without the fear of making mistakes.
