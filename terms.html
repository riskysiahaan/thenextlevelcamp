BOOKING & REFUND POLICY

Enrolment fee is non-transferable and non-refundable. All programs must be fully paid prior to the program’s commencement date. Late payment on scheduled payment dates shall result in participant not being admitted to the programme lesson and/or activities.

Once you have confirmed, no cancellations or refunds will be allowed, including for late arrival or early departure from camp or program unless a child is unable to participate due to an accident or illness per physician orders.

PROGRAM CLOSURE

Score Campus LLP reserves the full rights to postpone or cancel the programme or change the venue due to unforeseen circumstances. No refund payment shall be entertained should participants decide to withdraw from the programme. Students will be enrolled in the next available date.

Score Campus LLP reserves the full rights to reschedule programme time/day schedule, postpone or cancel any activities, replace trainers. Score Campus LLP reserves the full rights to revise the programme fee without prior notice for the future programme.

Strictly no make-up lessons for absence unless a medical certificate / hospitalisation letter is produced. Make-up lessons are subjected to approval by management of Score Campus LLP.

FIRST AID

In case of an emergency, and if a family physician cannot be reached, our staff reserves the right to make the decision without prior consent to be treated by Certified Emergency Personnel (i.e. EMT, First Responder, and/or Physician).

However, we aim to contact you as promptly as possible.

Please disclose any food and/or drug allergy your child has in advance. Send us an email at scorecampus@gmail.com if your child requires any medical condition that requires special attention from our camp staff and instructors.

TRAVEL AND INSURANCE

Participant’s passport must have at least 6 months’ validity from the date of departure for international camps.

Participants will have to get their own travel insurance. Score Campus LLP will take the public and accident liability insurance.

SPECIAL NEEDS & DISABILITY

Score Campus LLP welcomes children with disabilities and special needs. For more information on options and opportunities for participants with special needs or disabilities, please contact us at scorecampus@gmail.com.
